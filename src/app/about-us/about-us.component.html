Taj Mahal of India

Bringing the Flavors of India to Los Angeles

At Taj Mahal of India, food is more than a meal — it’s an experience. Inspired by the rich culinary traditions of India, we set out to create a place where guests can enjoy authentic flavors, warm hospitality, and the feeling of home with every visit.

Our Story

Founded with a passion for sharing India’s vibrant culture, Taj Mahal of India has become a place where tradition meets taste. Our chefs bring years of experience and a deep love for cooking, preparing each dish with the freshest ingredients, fragrant spices, and time-honored recipes passed down through generations.

Our Philosophy

Authenticity – We believe in serving dishes that honor the true flavors of India.

Quality – Every ingredient is carefully selected for freshness and taste.

Hospitality – Just like in India, every guest is welcomed as family

Our Food

From sizzling tandoori specialties and rich curries to comforting biryani and soft, freshly baked naan, every dish is crafted to transport you straight to India. Whether you’re vegetarian, vegan, or a meat lover, our menu offers something for everyone.
